# Proportion of points under quantile

Proportion of points under quantile.

## Usage

``` r
proportion_under_quantile(Y, Y.pred)
```

## Arguments

- Y:

  \\(N \times T)\\ matrix of observed time instants.

- Y.pred:

  \\(N \times T)\\ matrix of predicted time instants.

## Value

The proportion of points under the estimated quantile function
